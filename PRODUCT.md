# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Youth football players and their parents in Hanoi who are comparing local training and competitive match opportunities for boys and girls.

## Product Purpose

Present Drinkwater FC as a youth football club, explain its teams, schedule, coaching, and fees, and help prospective players arrange a trial or apply for membership.

## Brand Personality

Competitive, supportive, and direct. The club should feel serious about player development while remaining approachable to beginners and families.

## Anti-references

Do not position the club as a nonprofit, charity, fundraising organization, or community donation project. Avoid corporate sales language and unsupported claims about how membership fees are allocated.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with football, training, and player development.
2. Make team options, pricing, and schedules easy for families to understand.
3. Use concrete club details instead of institutional claims.
4. Keep the path from interest to trial session short and clear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep content readable on mobile and desktop, preserve keyboard-accessible links, maintain clear contrast, and avoid communicating important distinctions through color alone.
